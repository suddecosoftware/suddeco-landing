Common questions

How long does AI extraction take? ▼

Under 2 minutes for most drawing sets. A 15-page architectural PDF with multiple floors takes about 90 seconds. Complex 50+ page sets can take up to 4 minutes.

What drawing formats do you support? ▼

PDF is the primary format — any PDF from AutoCAD, Revit, Plan7Architect, or scanned drawings. Architectural, structural, and services drawings all supported.

Can my whole team use it? ▼

Yes. Add your QS, PM, site manager, and clients. Each role sees only what's relevant — clients never see your margins.

Is my project data secure? ▼

All data is stored on Azure UK South servers, compliant with UK GDPR. Your drawings and project data are never used to train AI models.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes, cancel from your account settings at any time. No contracts, no lock-in. Your data is available to export for 30 days after cancellation.
