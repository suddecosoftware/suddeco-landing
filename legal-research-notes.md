# Legal Research Notes

## HBXL Key Clauses Observed:
- General Disclaimer (no warranty on accuracy)
- Copyright (exclusive property, no reproduction)
- Limitation of Liability (capped, no consequential damages)
- Privacy & Personal Data (DPA 1998 reference - outdated, now UK GDPR)
- Hypertext Links (permission required)
- General terms (governing law, variation, notices)

## Key differences for Suddeco:
- HBXL terms are basic and somewhat outdated (references DPA 1998 instead of UK GDPR 2018)
- Suddeco needs modern UK GDPR compliance
- Suddeco is SaaS/cloud-based, needs specific clauses for:
  - Cloud service terms
  - AI-generated output disclaimers
  - Data processing and retention
  - Subscription and token-based billing
  - API usage terms
  - International IP protection (Berne Convention, WIPO)
  - Strong anti-copying provisions
  - GDPR lawful basis for processing
  - Data subject rights (access, erasure, portability)
  - Cookie policy
  - Third-party integrations
